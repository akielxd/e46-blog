Welcome to My BMW E46 Build Log#

This is my personal logbook for my 2004 BMW 325Ci, where I track every repair, modification, and milestone.

Whether you’re here for inspiration, how-to advice, or just to see the progress, this blog documents it all.

About This Project#

I picked up this 325Ci after missing the classic BMW straight-six character from my old E36 convertible.
Over time, I’ve fixed a lot of the initial issues (leaks, worn interior, cooling system), and now the car is ready for a build with clear goals.

Stay tuned — every update will be documented here with photos, notes, and results.
